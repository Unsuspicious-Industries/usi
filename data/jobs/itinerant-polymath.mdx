Here at USI, with deal with the **mystical** daily, an it usually cannot be tamed easily.

- Rapidly assess ambiguous problem spaces and propose concrete experiments, methods and protocols.
- Discuss with all departements, bridge ethics with profit, the future and the past.
- Leave behind clear documentation, failed experiments, weird ideas and ambiguous writtings.
