Outlines-style FSM masking is real and it works — but it only enforces *grammar*. A grammatically perfect, well-formed term can still be a type error, or a dead-end proof branch (Wittgenstein's *sinnlos*).

Mündler et al. push the constraint into the type system. P7 pushes one step further, into proof obligations. Same idea, escalating in strength.
